Before you suggest something

Please remember, the game is meant to be fun. Losing control of your player unexpectedly, being suddenly slowed down, or unable to use your abilities, typically isn’t fun (there’s a reason blink got nerfed to only last around 0.5s on players, and grapple requires being on top of the other player to tether them), please think about what you’re suggesting before sending it Also please do search for existing suggestions related to your idea, you may find something similar that has already been posted that you can add on to or react to instead of making a duplicate post. If your idea is sufficiently different to the existing ones you find, feel free to make a post, but maybe consider pointing out the differences between them so people don't just link you here

Extra Notes

Anything with a random component is generally not a good idea, if you really need to have something random, think about why, and what reactions it may cause in players. Rays should affect everything, or as close to everything as is reasonable. Transformations don't need to be complex, the existing ones of Drill, Rock, and Macho are all relatively intuitive, and don't require more than one button and the movement controls. (edited)

How To Make a Good Suggestion

  • Don't combine multiple things. Saying "I want voice chat, and also this ability, and also this mechanic, and also can you make the game free" is a terrible way to suggest things, which muddies the conversations being had in the thread.
  • No per-player screen effects. Remember that local play means all the players are using the same screen, so you can't have a visual effect apply to only one player's screen.
  • Side note, The 2.1.0 update has now set the precedent that visual effects can be different in local and online matches, but I'd still shy away from them
  • Remember that controllers exist, and your ability needs to be able to function without a specific mouse input, but rather an angle (think along the lines of a teleport to the mouse cursor, this wouldn't work for a controller, but an alternative could take inpiration form the Bloodied Scarf (https://enterthegungeon.fandom.com/wiki/Bloodied_Scarf) from Enter the Gungeon, being a charged ability that could either move outwards in the direction it was used, or create a controllable ghost player to teleport to when the ability is released)

Accessibility considerations

Just because you might not have a disability doesn't mean you should ignore them in your suggestion Big things include:
  • Flashing lights
  • Loud/intense sound effects
  • Changing other player's controls
  • Anything requiring extensive mashing, and ideally no holding down of buttons for a long time either
  • Common phobias (trypophobia, specific animals like spiders/snakes)
  • Complicated control schemes should be avoided where possible. The most complex abilities in the base game are probably Grapple, and Macho/Throw If you do have a particular disability or concern that isn't covered here, and especially so if it is a problem in the base game, please do mention it in the Accessibility Megathread (https://discord.com/channels/1091369043488936028/1189084963270819850)

If you would like to join any of the individual threads to know when a post is added to them, you can:

On PC

-# (web, the app probably works the same but I'm not sure) 1. Open the threads tab in this channel (It's located at the top right, to the left of the notifications and pinned messages tabs) 2. Right click (two finger click for a trackpad) on the thread you would like to join 3. Click the 2nd option, the one that says "Join Thread", just below the "Mark as Read" option. If this says "Leave Thread" you are already in it, and it will show up in your channel list when something is added

On Mobile

1. Click the channel name at the top of the screen 2. Open the Threads menu 3. Click on the Thread you want to join 4. Click the channel name for the Thread at the top of the screen 5. Open the Settings for the thread 6. Click "Notification Settings" 7. Select either "Only @mentions" or "All Messages" to join the thread (edited)
